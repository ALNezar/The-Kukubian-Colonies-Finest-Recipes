Blender Oatmeal Pancakes

Description

This oatmeal pancake recipe is EASY to make in the blender! These healthy pancakes are perfect for busy mornings. Whole grain and gluten-free! brekfest.

Blender Oatmeal Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 banana
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour small amounts of batter onto the pan.
  5. Cook for about 2 minutes on one side, then flip.
  6. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with your favorite toppings.
