Cinnamon Roll Oatmeal

Description

Cinnamon Roll Oatmeal is a creamy convenient bake-free recipe. The results are everything you love about cinnamon buns but in a chewy, hearty, and healthy package. snack.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats (gluten-free, if needed)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon cinnamon
  • 1 cup milk (any type, such as almond or dairy)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (or sweetener of choice)
  • 1 scoop protein powder (optional)
  • 1-2 tablespoons cream cheese

Steps

  1. Simmer: Combine all ingredients (except cream cheese) in a saucepan or microwave-safe bowl. Heat until most of the liquid has been absorbed. If using protein powder, stir it in at the end.
  2. Add cream cheese: Remove the oats from heat and stir in the cream cheese. Serve with extra cinnamon.

Tips & Variations

  • Extra protein: Swap cream cheese for Greek yogurt and add 1-2 tablespoons of powdered sugar (or a sugar substitute).
  • Adjust the liquid: If the oats are too thick, start with ½ cup of milk and add 1-2 tablespoons more as needed.
  • Increase the ratios: The base recipe uses a ½ cup of oats to 1 cup of milk. For every extra ½ cup of oats, increase the milk by ¾ cup. If you prefer thicker oatmeal, only increase by ½ cup.
